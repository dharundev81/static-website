CONFERENCE SPONSORSHIP PROPOSAL

ICPCIT 2018: INVITATION TO SPONSORS

About the International Conference of ICPCIT 2018

International Conference on Power, Circuit and Information Technology (ICPCIT-18) is a worldwide platform to promote scientific and educational activities towards the advancements of common man’s life by improving the theory and practice of various disciplines and sectors of engineering sciences. The attendance of more than 500 delegates is expected. ICPCIT-18 is going to be held on 16th – 17thMarch at Hyderabad, Telangana sponsored by Labtech innovations.

Theme of the Conference

The theme of the conference is to provide an international platform to all the world developers, researchers to engage in scientific discussion about current research and latest advancements in engineering and technology which facilitates the exchange of new ideas.

Conference Objective

The general aim of the conference is to promote the international collaboration in Education and Research in all fields and disciplines of engineering. Labtech innovations being one of the largest professional Association of South Asia organizes conferences, workshops, seminars and awareness programs and other supports to improve research and development activities.

WHY SPONSOR?

  Publicity and Long term branding.   Unique intellectual leadership communication opportunities.   Reaching the Scientists, Research Scholar and Indian Scientific Communities in India.   Promoting your Company, products and services.
