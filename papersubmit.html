Paper Submission

Author Guidelines


Papers are invited from research scholars, Faculty of colleges, PG students and Industries.

The manuscript should be 1.5 spacing, Arial 12 Font size and single column according to the .doc paper submission template (in .doc or .docx format) and to be submitted to the conference coordinator through info@iraj.co.in along with the Name, Date and Phone Numbers.

Maximum allowed pages while submitting camera ready paper is 6, extra pages will be charged with USD20/ INR200 for per page.

Paper should be in the format strictly specified on the conference website. Only the selected papers will get the intimation that will be accepted for publication. Kindly do not submit the paper multiple times; it may lead to cancellation of an article/paper.
